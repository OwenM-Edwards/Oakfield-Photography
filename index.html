A little bit about us..

"Oakfield Photography is a family run business, operating in and around Newport, Wales. Our beautiful studio is equipped to handle everything from baby and children shoots, to complex speciality shoots. We have wedding packages to suit all budgets and our style of photography makes every effort not to interfere with the normal flow of your day, adopting a more photojournalistic approach as much as possible."

"I have been professionally taking images since 1983, and have worked on a great variety of assingments. I was first trained in the British Army, before moving onto several further courses, including a 3-year course in professional photography with the New York Institute of Photography."
